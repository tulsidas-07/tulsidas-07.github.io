Privacy Policy

Official Data Policy for Tulsidas Dev Studio Applications

At Tulsidas Dev Studio, we believe in transparency and user privacy. This Privacy Policy governs all mobile applications published under our developer account. We do not sell your data, and we only use industry-standard services to provide a high-quality user experience.

1. Data Collection & Third-Party Services

Our applications do not directly collect personal data like your name, address, or phone number. We utilize trusted third-party SDKs for app functionality and monetization, which may collect device-specific information (such as Advertising IDs):

  • Google Play Services: Required for core Android functionality and security.
  • AdMob (Google): Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect data to serve relevant ads to users.
  • Firebase Analytics: Helps us understand app usage patterns to improve performance and fix crashes.

2. Advertising & User Choice

We use advertisements to keep our applications free for everyone. Users can manage their ad preferences or reset their Advertising Identifier within their Android device settings (Settings > Google > Ads). For users in regions with specific privacy laws (like GDPR), we provide a consent mechanism to manage your data choices.

3. Children’s Privacy

Tulsidas Dev Studio applications are intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have accidentally received information from a child, please contact us immediately.

4. Information Security

We take all reasonable steps to protect any technical data transmitted between the app and third-party services. However, please be aware that no electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally to reflect changes in our apps or legal requirements. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
